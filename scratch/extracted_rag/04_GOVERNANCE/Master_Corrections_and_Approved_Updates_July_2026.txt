1. Spa Operating Hours (resolves CON-005)
Weekdays (Monday-Friday): 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM. Last treatment start time: 7:45 PM for 60-minute treatments, 7:15
PM for 90-minute treatments.
Weekends and public holidays: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM. Last treatment start time: 8:45 PM for 60-minute treatments,
8:15 PM for 90-minute treatments.
This extends, and does not contradict, the base hours in Spa and Activities.pdf. Reception hours extend
correspondingly on weekends (8:00 AM - 10:00 PM).
2. Infinity Pool Description (resolves CON-007)
The approved guest-facing description is: "outdoor, temperature-moderated infinity pool with a valley outlook."
The pool is not described as "heated" - its temperature is passively moderated and varies with weather and
operational settings, consistent with Amenities_and_Facilities_RKPR_Resort.pdf and the Room Catalogue's
description of villa pools. Any reference to a "heated infinity pool" (e.g. on the public website, which is outside this
document set) is incorrect and has been flagged to the Marketing team.
3. Kids' Club Closing Time (resolves CON-009 and CON-012)
The Kids' Club closes at 7:00 PM daily, with last entry at 6:30 PM. This is confirmed by Resort Directory and
Timmings.pdf (the master timing source per CON-001) and by Amenities_and_Facilities_RKPR_Resort.pdf.
Two other values have circulated and are both superseded: an old Welcome Letter stated 8:00 PM, and the
Resort Activities schedule table in Spa and Activities Images.pdf stated 6:00 PM. Neither is correct as a standing
rule.
On declared long weekends and festival periods, the Kids' Club may extend to 8:00 PM, but this requires
same-day staffing confirmation from the Recreation Manager and must not be quoted to guests as guaranteed.
4. Spa Name (resolves CON-011)
The spa's current and only official name is Aranya Wellness Spa. "Cedar Leaf Spa" was the resort's spa name
before the 2026 rebrand and must not be used in guest-facing answers. Resort_FAQ.txt has been corrected
accordingly.
