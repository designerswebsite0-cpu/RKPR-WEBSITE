1. General Accommodation Information
RKPR Resort offers a collection of luxury rooms, suites, and private villas for couples, families, groups, and guests 
requiring accessible accommodation. The information below outlines the standard accommodation features, occupancy 
limits, bedding arrangements, views, accessibility provisions, and other important stay details. Room availability, rates, 
taxes, and package inclusions vary by date and booking plan.
Property standard: Luxury five-star leisure resort
Default check-in: 2:00 PM
Default check-out: 11:00 AM
Accommodation policy: All indoor guest rooms and villas are non-smoking
Breakfast standard: Daily breakfast is included for the number of registered guests covered by the booked rate plan, 
unless the selected offer is explicitly marked room-only
Occupancy principle: Maximum occupancy limits are strict and include adults and children
Child age bands: Infant: 0-5 years; Child: 6-11 years; Adult: 12 years and above
Extra-bed rule: Only categories specifically marked as extra-bed eligible may receive a rollaway bed
Accessibility rule: Accessible inventory must be requested at booking and remains subject to availability

5.1 Garden Deluxe Room
Category code: GDR | Resort inventory: 24 rooms
An elegant entry-level luxury room located in the resort's low-rise garden wings. The room opens toward landscaped 
lawns, flowering plants, and shaded walking paths. It is quiet, convenient, and suitable for guests who value easy access 
to the lobby and dining areas.
Ideal for: Couples, solo travellers, short leisure stays, and senior guests who prefer proximity to central facilities
Room size: 42 sq m / 452 sq ft, including bathroom and balcony
Bed configuration: One king bed. Twin beds are available in a limited number of rooms and must be requested before 
arrival.
Maximum occupancy: Maximum 2 adults and 1 child. A third adult is not permitted.
Children policy: One child aged 0-5 may share the existing bed without an additional sleeping charge. One child aged 6-
11 may stay using a rollaway bed at the applicable charge.
View type: Landscaped garden view; no guaranteed mountain or valley view
Balcony, patio, or deck: Private furnished balcony with two chairs and a side table
Private pool: No private pool
Jacuzzi: No jacuzzi
Bathroom: Walk-in rain shower, single vanity, enclosed toilet, premium toiletries, bathrobes, slippers, hair dryer
Category-specific amenities: 43-inch smart TV; minibar refrigerator; tea and coffee station; writing desk; electronic safe; 
blackout curtains; umbrella; universal sockets and USB charging
Breakfast inclusion: Included when booked on the standard breakfast-inclusive rate. Room-only promotional rates may 
exclude breakfast and must be stated at booking.
Extra-bed and baby-cot availability: One rollaway bed is available only for a child aged 6-11. Baby cot available subject 
to availability. No rollaway for a third adult.
Accessibility: Four Garden Deluxe Rooms are designated wheelchair accessible. These have step-free access, wider 
entrance and bathroom doors, grab bars, lowered switches, a roll-in shower, and an emergency pull cord. The accessible 
layout has one king bed only.
Connecting-room availability: Eight rooms form four connecting pairs. Each pair consists of one king room and one 
twin room. Connecting rooms are request-based and cannot be guaranteed until confirmed by reservations.
Smoking status: Non-smoking indoors. Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor smoking zones, not on 
balconies.
Location within the resort: Garden Wings A and B, approximately 1-3 minutes' walk from the lobby and 2-4 minutes 
from the all-day dining restaurant.
Additional information: This is the most convenient category for guests with limited walking tolerance, but only the 
specifically designated accessible rooms include full accessibility adaptations.

6. Differences Between Accommodation Categories
Garden Deluxe vs Valley View Premium
The Garden Deluxe is closer to central facilities and has a garden view. The Valley View Premium is larger, guarantees a 
valley view, permits a third adult, and includes a bathtub.
Valley View Premium vs Mountain Panorama Suite
The Valley room has one open sleeping area and a valley view. The Mountain Panorama Suite has a separate living 
room, guaranteed mountain view, larger balcony, and private indoor whirlpool jacuzzi.
Mountain Panorama Suite vs Honeymoon Pool Villa
The suite accepts a small family and has no pool. The Honeymoon Pool Villa is adults-only, has a private heated plunge 
pool and greater privacy, but no separate living room.
Family Courtyard Suite vs Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa
The Family Suite has one main bedroom plus children's sleeping space and no private pool. The Grand Villa has two full 
bedrooms, a private pool and jacuzzi, and accommodates up to seven guests.
Honeymoon Pool Villa vs Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa
Both have private pools. The Honeymoon Villa is for exactly two adults and prioritises romance and seclusion. The Grand 
Villa is designed for families or groups and includes two bedrooms, a large pool, and an outdoor jacuzzi.
7. Choosing the Right Accommodation
 The Family Courtyard Suite is the most spacious standard option for a family of four. The Valley View Premium Room 
and Mountain Panorama Suite can also accommodate two adults and two children.
 Three adults may stay in the Valley View Premium Room, Mountain Panorama Suite, or Grand Two-Bedroom Pool 
Villa. The Garden Deluxe Room and Honeymoon Pool Villa do not accommodate three adults.
 The Honeymoon Pool Villa offers a private plunge pool for two adults. The Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa offers a 
larger private pool for families and groups.
 Guaranteed mountain views are available in the Mountain Panorama Suite and Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa.
 The Valley View Premium Room offers a guaranteed valley view without a private pool.
 Wheelchair-accessible accommodation is available in four designated Garden Deluxe Rooms and must be requested 
in advance.
 The Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa is the only category with two separate full bedrooms.
 Connecting rooms, twin beds, a particular floor, an exact view angle, early check-in, and late check-out are subject to 
availability and require confirmation.
 A standard bathtub or soaking tub is different from a jacuzzi. Jacuzzis are available only in the Mountain Panorama 
Suite and Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa.
 Standard rates generally include breakfast, while room-only and promotional plans may have different inclusions. The 
selected rate plan should be checked before booking.
8. Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Which rooms have private pools?
A: The Honeymoon Pool Villa has a private heated plunge pool for two adults. The Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa has a 
larger private temperature-controlled swimming pool and an outdoor jacuzzi.
Q: Can three adults stay in the Garden Deluxe Room?
A: No. Its maximum occupancy is two adults and one child. Three adults should book a Valley View Premium Room, 
Mountain Panorama Suite, or a larger villa.

Q: Which room has a mountain view?
A: The Mountain Panorama Suite guarantees a panoramic mountain view. The Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa also offers 
mountain-and-valley views.
Q: Does the villa include breakfast?
A: The resort's standard villa rates include breakfast for the registered guests covered by the rate. A room-only or special 
promotional rate may differ, so the final booking confirmation should be checked.
Q: Which room is suitable for a family of four?
A: The Family Courtyard Suite is the strongest recommendation. A Valley View Premium Room or Mountain Panorama 
Suite can also accommodate two adults and two children.
Q: Do you have wheelchair-accessible rooms?
A: Yes. Four Garden Deluxe Rooms are specifically adapted with step-free access, wider doors, grab bars, lowered 
controls, and a roll-in shower. They must be requested and confirmed in advance.
Q: Can I book connecting rooms?
A: RKPR Resort has connecting pairs in the Garden Deluxe and Valley View Premium categories. They are limited and 
must be confirmed by reservations.
Q: Are all rooms non-smoking?
A: Yes. All indoor rooms, villas, balconies, patios, decks, and private gardens are non-smoking. Guests may use the 
resort's designated outdoor smoking areas.
Q: Can children stay in the Honeymoon Pool Villa?
A: No. The Honeymoon Pool Villa accommodates a maximum of two adults and does not accept children, infants, baby 
cots, or extra beds.
Q: Which category has a jacuzzi?
A: The Mountain Panorama Suite has a private indoor whirlpool jacuzzi for two. The Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villa has 
an outdoor whirlpool jacuzzi for four.
Q: Can I request twin beds?
A: Twin beds are available in selected Garden Deluxe and Valley View Premium rooms, and in the second bedroom of 
selected Grand Two-Bedroom Pool Villas. The request must be made before arrival and is subject to confirmation.
Q: Can an extra bed be added?
A: A rollaway bed is available in selected categories according to their occupancy rules. It is not available in the 
Honeymoon Pool Villa or Family Courtyard Suite.
