1. Spa Information
1.1 Spa Overview
Spa name: Aranya Wellness Spa
Location: Wellness Wing, beside the infinity pool
Opening time: 9:00 AM
Closing time: 9:00 PM
Last treatment start time: 7:45 PM for 60-minute treatments and 7:15 PM for 90-minute treatments
Reception hours: 8:30 AM-9:00 PM
Treatment rooms: 6 single treatment rooms and 2 couple treatment suites
Therapist availability: Subject to daily roster, guest preference, treatment type and advance booking
Minimum guest age: 16 years for standard massage treatments; selected teen treatments available from age 13 with parental consent
Advance booking: Strongly recommended, especially between 4:00 PM and 8:00 PM, on weekends and during peak season
1.2 Spa Booking and Cancellation Rules
Guests should arrive at least 15 minutes before the appointment.
First-time guests must complete a wellness consultation form.
Treatments may be shortened if a guest arrives late, while the full treatment charge remains applicable.
Cancellations made at least 6 hours before the appointment are free of charge.
Cancellations made within 6 hours attract a charge equal to 50% of the treatment value.
No-shows and cancellations within 2 hours attract a charge equal to 100% of the treatment value.
Couple treatments must be cancelled at least 12 hours in advance to avoid charges.
Group spa bookings of 5 or more guests must be cancelled at least 24 hours in advance.
Appointments may be rescheduled once without charge if notice is provided within the applicable cancellation deadline.
1.3 Dress and Preparation Instructions
Robes, slippers and disposable undergarments are provided.
Guests should avoid heavy meals and alcohol for at least 2 hours before treatment.
Jewellery and valuables should be left in the room safe.
Swimwear is required for the steam room, sauna and hydrotherapy jacuzzi.
Guests should shower before using wet facilities.
Mobile phones must remain on silent mode throughout the spa.
1.4 Medical Restrictions
Guests must inform the spa team of:
Pregnancy or recent childbirth
High or low blood pressure

2. Activities Information
2.1 General Activity Rules
Activity schedules may change because of weather, maintenance, instructor availability or private events.
Guests must report at the stated meeting point at least 10 minutes before departure.
Closed footwear is mandatory for cycling, trekking, archery and adventure activities.
Helmets and basic safety equipment are provided where required.
Guests must disclose medical conditions, recent injuries and pregnancy before participation.
The resort may refuse participation when a guest appears unfit, intoxicated or unable to follow safety instructions.
Children must remain within the published age range and supervision rules.
Activities marked complimentary are included for registered resort guests only.
Paid activities are charged to the guest room or settled before participation.
Weather-related cancellation by the resort results in a full refund or rescheduling.
Guest cancellations after the stated deadline may attract the full charge.

2.10 Activity Cancellation Rules
Complimentary activities should be cancelled at least 1 hour before start time.
Paid activities under INR 1,000 should be cancelled at least 2 hours before start time.
Paid activities of INR 1,000 or more should be cancelled at least 6 hours before start time.
Treks, mountain cycling and high-rope activities require cancellation by the published previous-day deadline.
No-shows and late cancellations may be charged at 100%.
Weather cancellation by the resort results in rescheduling or a full refund.
If an activity starts but is stopped for safety after more than half of its scheduled duration, no refund is normally due.
3. Frequently Asked Questions
Is the spa open in the evening?
Yes. Aranya Wellness Spa is open until 9:00 PM. The last treatment start time depends on treatment duration.
How much does a couple massage cost?
The Mountain Serenity for Two costs INR 11,800 for two guests. Other couple rituals range up to INR 18,500 for two.
Are there activities for children?
Yes. The Kids’ Club, arts and crafts, treasure hunts, junior chef workshops, storytelling, movie nights and nature-explorer sessions are
available for children within the stated age limits.

--- Page 10 ---
Is the nature walk free?
The daily Estate Nature Walk and Sunset View Walk are complimentary for registered guests. Specialist walks such as birdwatching and
forest interpretation are chargeable.
Do I need to book yoga in advance?
Yes. Sunrise Yoga should be booked by 9:00 PM on the previous day. Sunset Yoga and Aqua Yoga also require advance booking.
Will the bonfire happen if it rains?
No. The bonfire is cancelled during rain, strong wind or fire restrictions. An indoor evening program may be offered instead.
Can children use the spa?
Standard massages are available from age 16. Selected teen treatments are available from age 13 with parental consent.
Are sauna and steam facilities complimentary?
They are complimentary for guests receiving a spa treatment on the same day. Facility-only access may be charged.
Do I need previous experience for cycling?
Leisure cycling requires basic cycling ability. Mountain cycling requires good fitness and previous confidence on a bicycle.
What happens if weather cancels a paid activity?
The activity is rescheduled or fully refunded.
