1. Payment Policy Overview
RKPR Resort accepts approved digital and in-person payment methods in Indian Rupees ( ₹ ). A reservation becomes
confirmed only after the required payment is received and verified by the payment gateway or Finance Team.
A booking may remain tentative while payment is pending, under verification or partially received. Tentative bookings
may expire if the required payment is not completed by the stated deadline.
Guests may be informed about payment methods, advance requirements, billing timelines, refund timelines, invoice
availability and safety rules. Refund approval, fee waivers, price changes, compensation, manual confirmation and
disputed payments require authorised staff.

3. Reservation Advance Requirements
Standard reservations
A 30% advance is required to confirm a standard room reservation.
The balance is payable before arrival or during check-in, as stated in the booking confirmation.
The booking remains tentative until the advance is verified.
Standard payment links generally remain valid for 6 hours.
Peak-season reservations
Peak season includes 20 December to 5 January, selected public holidays, festival weekends and announced long
weekends.
Peak-season reservations may require:
100% advance payment
Stricter cancellation terms
Non-refundable or partially refundable rates
Mandatory meal supplements on selected dates
Last-minute reservations
Bookings made within 24 hours of arrival may require full payment before confirmation. Same-day and walk-in
reservations must be paid according to availability and the rate plan offered at that time.
Group, wedding and event reservations
These normally require:
Initial booking deposit
Milestone-based payments
Final payment before the event or group arrival
Separate damage or security deposit
Signed contract with cancellation and rescheduling terms
4. Payment Deadlines and Booking Expiry
If the payment deadline passes without successful verification:
The booking may expire automatically.
Held inventory may be released.
The original price and room availability are no longer guaranteed.

Deadline extensions require staff approval.
5. Payment Link Procedure
Official payment links may be sent through:
The guest’s registered email
The registered WhatsApp number
The secure RKPR Resort booking website
Before paying, the guest should verify the resort name, booking reference, amount, guest name and secure payment
page.
The resort will never request:
OTP
Card PIN
CVV
Full card number
UPI PIN
Banking password
A payment screenshot is supporting evidence only. It does not confirm payment until the resort system or Finance Team
verifies the transaction.
6. Payment Verification and Statuses
Common payment statuses are:
Payment initiated
Payment pending
Payment successful
Payment failed
Payment under verification
Payment partially received
Payment refunded
Payment partially refunded
Payment disputed
A bank debit message does not always mean RKPR Resort received the funds. A booking is confirmed only when
payment is marked successful and verified.
Guests reporting payment issues should provide the booking reference, transaction ID, payment date, amount and
payment method.

8. Security Deposit
Selected premium villas may require a refundable security deposit of ₹ 5,000. Group and event bookings may require a
higher deposit.
The deposit may be collected before arrival or during check-in. Refund is subject to room inspection and settlement of
outstanding charges.
Possible deductions include:
Property damage
Missing items
Excessive cleaning
Smoking violations
Unpaid dining, spa, transfer or activity charges
Event damage
Lost access cards or keys
Approved deposit refunds are initiated within 7 business days. Bank processing time may be additional.

10. Final Bill and Checkout Settlement
Before checkout, the resort prepares the final bill and adjusts:
Advance payments
Posted services
Applicable taxes
Security deposit
Outstanding balance
Guests may normally use one or more approved cards, UPI, cash within legal limits, or a permitted combination. Split
payment should be requested before final settlement.
If a guest disputes a charge, the item is reviewed by the relevant department. If the billing system is temporarily
unavailable, a provisional statement may be provided and the official invoice issued later.

13. Refund Policy
Refunds require approval and are normally returned to the original payment method.
Approved refunds are initiated within 7–10 business days.
International-card refunds may take longer.
Banks and gateways may require additional processing time.
Online card or UPI payments are not normally refunded in cash.
Non-refundable bookings remain subject to their confirmed rate conditions.
The resort provides a refund reference once available.
14. Cancellation-Related Payments
The refund amount depends on:
Rate plan
Cancellation date
Advance amount
Peak-season conditions
No-show status
Early departure
Rescheduling request
Package or event terms
Medical, emergency and force-majeure exceptions require authorised approval. Standard policy should be explained
without promising a waiver.
15. Failed Payments
Common causes include bank decline, insufficient balance, UPI timeout, expired link, network failure, gateway error and
international-card restrictions.
The guest may:
1. Check the bank status.
2. Wait briefly before retrying.
3. Request a fresh payment link.
4. Use another approved method.
5. Contact the issuing bank.
Sensitive credentials must never be shared.
16. Pending Payments
When an amount is deducted but the status remains pending:

Avoid making repeated payments until advised.
Wait up to 30 minutes for gateway reconciliation.
Share the booking reference and transaction ID.
Finance may raise an investigation if the payment remains unresolved.
The booking remains tentative until payment is verified.
17. Duplicate Payments
Duplicate-payment process:
1. Guest reports both transaction references.
2. Booking and settlement records are checked.
3. Finance verifies whether both payments reached the resort.
4. Any confirmed excess amount is refunded or adjusted after approval.
An immediate refund must not be promised before verification.
18. Partial Payments
Partial payment is allowed only when permitted by the rate plan, contract or approved payment schedule. The minimum
required advance must be received by the stated deadline.
Missing a group or event payment milestone may result in cancellation, revised pricing or release of reserved inventory.
19. Bank Transfers
Bank transfers require manual verification. Guests must mention the booking reference and use only bank details supplied
through an authorised resort quotation or payment instruction.
Account name: RKPR Resort
Bank details: Available only through an official authorised communication.
Weekend, holiday, interbank and international transfers may take longer. Transfer charges and short payments are the
guest’s responsibility unless otherwise agreed.
20. International Payments
Selected international cards are accepted. Transactions settle in Indian Rupees. The guest’s bank may apply foreign
transaction or currency-conversion fees.
International transfers and refunds may take longer. Identification may be required for certain high-value transactions.
21. Travel-Agent and Third-Party Bookings
OTA and travel-agent bookings may be prepaid, pay-at-property or voucher-based. The resort can only modify charges
and terms that fall within its agreement with the platform or agent.

23. Payment Disputes and Corrections
For an incorrect or unrecognised charge, collect:
Booking reference
Invoice number
Transaction ID
Date and amount
Description of the disputed item
Finance and the relevant operating department will review the evidence before correcting or refunding a charge.
24. Payment Safety and Fraud Prevention
Guests should:
Pay only through approved resort channels.
Avoid personal bank accounts shared by unknown individuals.
Verify the sender, amount and payment domain.
Use QR codes only when issued through an official channel.
Hide sensitive information in screenshots.
Report suspicious payment requests immediately.
The resort never asks for OTPs, PINs or CVVs.

27. Frequently Asked Payment Questions
Q: How much advance is required?
A: Standard reservations require a 30% verified advance.
Q: Do you accept UPI?
A: Yes, through approved online and resort payment channels.
Q: Can I pay at check-in?
A: The remaining balance may be paid at check-in when the confirmed rate plan permits it.
Q: Is my booking confirmed after I send a screenshot?
A: No. Confirmation requires successful payment verification.
Q: Are taxes included?
A: The booking summary states whether taxes are included. The official invoice shows the final tax breakdown.
Q: Is there a security deposit?
A: Selected premium villas may require a refundable ₹ 5,000 deposit.

--- Page 10 ---
Q: Can I split payment between two cards?
A: Usually yes, when requested before final settlement.
Q: Can I get a GST invoice?
A: Yes, when complete GST and billing details are provided before final billing.
Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Approved refunds are initiated within 7–10 business days.
Q: My payment was deducted but is pending. What should I do?
A: Do not pay again immediately. Share the booking reference and transaction ID for verification.
Q: What happens if I paid twice?
A: Finance verifies both transactions and refunds or adjusts any confirmed excess payment.
Q: Can I use an international card?
A: Selected international cards are accepted, subject to bank approval.
Q: Can I pay in cash?
A: Yes, at the resort within applicable legal limits.
Q: Can the cancellation fee be waived?
A: Only authorised staff may approve an exception.
Q: Can someone else pay for my booking?
A: Yes, but the payment must be linked to the correct guest and booking reference.
Q: Can I change the payment method after booking?
A: The unpaid balance may use another approved method. A completed payment is not simply moved between methods.
Q: Can I receive separate invoices?
A: Yes, when requested before checkout and permitted by billing rules.
Q: Are restaurant and spa charges added to the room?
A: They may be posted to the room folio when room-charging is authorised.
Q: What happens if my card fails at checkout?
A: Another approved payment method must be used.
Q: Can a refund be sent to another bank account?
A: Refunds normally return to the original payment method.
