PRICING STATUS: APPROVED. All prices in this document (room tariffs, extra-person/meal-plan charges, spa and
transport summaries, activity charges and event/wedding packages) are approved official RKPR Resort pricing for
the 1 Jan - 31 Dec 2026 validity period, signed off by the Finance Manager on 17 July 2026. This supersedes the
earlier draft status recorded in Pending_Pricing_SignOff_Tracker.xlsx (STAFF_ONLY), which is retained only as a
historical sign-off record.
All prices are in Indian Rupees (INR) and are inclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise stated. Rates are per room/villa per
night unless stated as per-person. Rates are indicative and subject to change; the confirmed booking rate is the one stated on
the guest's official reservation confirmation.

Important Notes
 Rates are subject to change without prior notice for unconfirmed/unbooked dates. Confirmed reservations are
governed by the rate stated on the booking confirmation.
 Long weekends and festive dates will be charged according to High Season/Peak Season pricing.
 Check-in time: 2:00 PM | Check-out time: 11:00 AM. Early check-in / late check-out is subject to availability and
may incur additional charges.
 A refundable security deposit (from INR 5,000, higher for villas, groups and events) may be collected at check-in -
see Payment and Billing.pdf.
 This rate card is the single authoritative pricing source for room, meal-plan, spa-summary, transport-summary,
laundry, cancellation-summary and event-package pricing. Where any other document appears to disagree with a
price shown here, this document controls and the discrepancy should be logged in
Conflicting_Information_Register.xlsx.
