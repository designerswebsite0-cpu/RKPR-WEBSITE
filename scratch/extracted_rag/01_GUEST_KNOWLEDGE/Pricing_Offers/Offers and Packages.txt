1. General Package Conditions
Package prices are per room or villa unless specifically stated otherwise.
Applicable taxes are included only where expressly mentioned.
All offers are subject to room inventory, blackout dates and package-specific eligibility rules.
Package benefits have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for other services unless approved in writing.
Unused inclusions are non-refundable and cannot be carried forward.
Offers cannot be combined with another promotion, contracted rate, loyalty benefit or corporate rate unless
explicitly permitted.
Check-in begins at 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM.
Children aged 0–5 stay complimentary on existing bedding unless the package says otherwise.
Children aged 6–11 are charged as children. Guests aged 12 and above are charged as adults.
Additional guests, extra beds, upgraded meals, premium activities and transport are charged separately unless
included.
Package validity refers to stay dates, not merely booking dates.
Management may withdraw or modify an offer before confirmation. Confirmed reservations remain governed by
the issued package terms.

13. Booking and Verification Procedure
1. Confirm stay dates, number of guests and preferred room category.
2. Check whether the package is active for the requested dates.
3. Verify minimum-stay requirements and blackout dates.
4. Confirm guest eligibility, child ages and occupancy limits.
5. Apply the correct booking code.
6. Issue a written confirmation listing the inclusions, exclusions, price and cancellation terms.
7. Record the terms version as OP-2026.1.
8. Collect payment or deposit according to the package conditions.
9. Reserve included dining, spa, transport or activity services where advance slots are required.
14. Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Can I combine a package with another offer?
A: No, unless the written package terms specifically allow combination.
Q: Are taxes included in the package price?
A: Taxes are included only where expressly stated in the confirmation. The final quotation shows the complete payable
amount.
Q: What happens if I do not use an included spa treatment or dinner?
A: Unused benefits are non-refundable and cannot normally be exchanged or carried forward.

--- Page 13 ---
Q: Can I change my room category after booking a package?
A: Yes, subject to availability, package eligibility and payment of the applicable upgrade difference.
Q: Are packages available during Christmas and New Year?
A: Most standard packages are unavailable from 24 December to 2 January unless a special festive package is issued.
Q: Do children receive all package inclusions?
A: Child benefits depend on the specific package. Adult spa treatments, wellness sessions and certain activities are not
automatically extended to children.
Q: Is the booking code required?
A: Yes. The correct booking code should appear on the reservation and confirmation to ensure the offer is applied
properly.
Q: Can an expired package be honoured?
A: No, unless management approves it in writing before confirmation.
