--- Table 8 ---
Field | Details
Tracked flight delay | When a valid flight number is provided, the driver monitors the flight and adjusts pickup to the revised arrival time
No charge for flight delay | No waiting charge applies during the included waiting allowance when the delay is reflected in official flight tracking
Delayed baggage or immigration | The guest should notify the driver. Waiting beyond the included allowance is charged at INR 600 per hour for sedan/SUV/MUV, INR 900 per hour for van and INR 1,500 per hour for luxury vehicles
Flight diversion or cancellation | The transfer is held or rescheduled where practical. Additional vehicle deployment costs may apply
Incorrect flight information | Waiting and rescheduling charges may apply when the flight number, terminal or arrival date supplied by the guest is incorrect
No communication | If the guest cannot be contacted for 120 minutes after actual landing, the transfer may be treated as a no-show

--- Table 17 ---
Question | Answer
Can the resort arrange airport pickup? | Yes. Airport pickup is available 24 hours with advance confirmation.
How much does airport transfer cost? | A one-way transfer from Bengaluru Airport starts at INR 8,500 for a Premium Sedan. Prices vary by vehicle.
Which airport is closest? | Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru is the nearest major airport, approximately 190 km away.
Can six people travel in one vehicle? | Yes, in a Luxury MUV if luggage fits within the stated allowance. With heavy luggage, a Tempo Traveller is recommended.
What happens if my flight is delayed? | The driver tracks the flight using the supplied flight number and adjusts the pickup time. Standard waiting allowances apply after actual landing.
Can I arrange a pickup at midnight? | Yes. Midnight pickup is available, but the applicable night charge is added.
