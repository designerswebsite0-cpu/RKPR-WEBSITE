6. In-Room Dining
In-room dining operates 24 hours. A midnight menu (abbreviated selection from Azure Terrace and The Cedar
Lounge) is served from 11:00 PM to 6:30 AM. A tray/trolley delivery charge of INR 150 applies outside standard
restaurant hours (11:00 PM - 6:30 AM); no delivery charge applies during standard hours. Full Azure Terrace and
Ember & Spice menus are available for in-room dining during their respective operating hours.

8. Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Do you serve Jain food?
A: Yes. Jain dishes are available across all dining venues with advance notice for certain preparations.
Q: Do you have vegan and gluten-free options?
A: Yes. Vegan and gluten-free items are marked on this menu (VG/VG-A and GF/GFA). Please inform the
restaurant of dietary requirements.
Q: What time does room service operate?
A: 24 hours, with a midnight menu from 11:00 PM to 6:30 AM.
Q: Is alcohol available?
A: Yes, at Summit Bar and via in-room dining, served only to guests 21 and above with valid ID.
Q: Which restaurant is best for a family dinner?
A: Azure Terrace offers the widest variety and a Kids' Menu. Ember & Spice is recommended for an adult-focused
Indian grill dinner.
