1. General Notes
All timings use Indian Standard Time.
Operating hours may change because of maintenance, weather, private events, government instructions, public
holidays or seasonal demand.
Last-entry and last-order times are strictly observed unless management approves an exception.
Facilities shown as “24 hours” remain available throughout the day, but response or delivery times may vary
overnight.
Guests should contact the listed extension for same-day confirmation during festivals, long weekends or severe
weather.

9. Holiday Exceptions
Christmas Eve and Christmas Day: Restaurant and entertainment timings may be extended. Some outlets may
operate fixed festive menus.
New Year’s Eve: Restaurants, bar and entertainment venues may operate later than usual. Access may require a
confirmed event booking.
New Year’s Day: Breakfast may be extended until 11:00 AM.
Public holidays and long weekends: Kids’ Club, recreation and transport services may experience higher
demand and require advance booking.

--- Page 6 ---
Private events: Event lawns, meeting rooms, private dining areas or selected public facilities may close
temporarily.
Maintenance: Swimming pools, spa wet areas, lifts or technical facilities may close temporarily for inspection or
repair.
10. Seasonal Schedule
Monsoon Schedule
Outdoor cycling, treks, nature trails, bonfires and cultural programmes may be cancelled, shortened or moved
indoors.
Pool operations may pause during thunderstorms or poor visibility.
Spa, gym, indoor games, library and business facilities continue normally unless maintenance is required.
Summer Schedule
Main pool hours may be extended until 9:30 PM on selected high-occupancy dates.
Hydration breaks may be added to outdoor activities.
Afternoon outdoor activities may be rescheduled to cooler hours.
Winter Schedule
Bonfire and evening cultural programmes may operate more frequently.
Early-morning outdoor activities may begin later during heavy fog or low visibility.
Pool water temperature and operating hours remain subject to weather conditions.

12. Frequently Asked Timing Questions
Q: What time does breakfast end?
A: Breakfast at Azure Terrace ends at 10:30 AM. The last buffet entry is 10:15 AM.

--- Page 7 ---
Q: Is the swimming pool open in the evening?
A: Yes. The infinity pool is open until 9:00 PM, with last entry at 8:45 PM.
Q: Is the spa open in the evening?
A: Yes. Cedar Leaf Spa is open until 9:00 PM. The final treatment normally begins at 8:00 PM.
Q: Is the gym open early in the morning?
A: Yes. The Fitness Centre opens at 6:00 AM.
Q: What time does the Kids’ Club close?
A: The Kids’ Club closes at 7:00 PM. Last entry is at 6:30 PM.
Q: Is room service available at midnight?
A: Yes. A reduced midnight menu is available from 11:00 PM to 6:30 AM.
Q: Is the Front Office open all night?
A: Yes. Front Office, Concierge, Security, Medical Assistance and the Transport Desk operate 24 hours.
Q: Will timings change on holidays?
A: They may. Restaurants, entertainment and family activities may have extended or revised schedules during festivals and
public holidays.
