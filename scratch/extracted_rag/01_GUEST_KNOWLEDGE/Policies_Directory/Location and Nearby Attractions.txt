Local transport options
Resort-arranged car: Recommended for airport, railway-station and sightseeing transfers.
Private taxi: Available from Mysuru and Chamarajanagar; return transport should be confirmed in advance.
Local taxi: Limited in Yelandur and nearby villages, especially after 7:00 PM.
App-based taxis: Availability near the resort is unreliable.
Self-drive: Suitable for confident drivers comfortable with winding hill roads and reduced night visibility.
Two-wheelers: Not recommended after dark because of wildlife movement and limited roadside lighting.

4.2 Biligiri Ranganatha Swamy Temple
Field Details
Description Historic hilltop temple associated with the Biligiriranga Hills and local pilgrimage traditions.
Distance Approximately 21 km
Travel time 40-50 minutes
Typical visiting hoursMorning and evening temple periods; timings may change on festival days.
Entry fee No standard entry fee; donations are optional.
Best visiting period October-March; early morning for cooler conditions.
Transport Resort car recommended.
Accessibility Hilltop approach and temple steps may be difficult for guests with limited mobility.
Dress and conduct Modest clothing and respectful behaviour required. Footwear must be removed.
Seasonal warning Festival days can be crowded and road access may be controlled.

4.3 K. Gudi Forest Area
Field Details
Description Forest-edge area known for wildlife habitat, birdlife and guided nature experiences.
Distance Approximately 28 km
Travel time 50-60 minutes
Timings Forest-entry and activity timings are controlled by the relevant forest authorities.
Entry fee Depends on the activity, vehicle and authority-approved rates.
Best visiting periodOctober-May; mornings and late afternoons offer better wildlife activity.
Transport Approved vehicle or resort-arranged car to the designated access point.

7. Accessibility and Safety Notes
Attraction accessibility varies and cannot be guaranteed because several sites have steps, slopes, sand, forest
terrain or heritage-building restrictions.
Guests requiring wheelchair access should inform the concierge before booking an itinerary.

--- Page 8 ---
Night driving on forest-edge roads should be avoided where possible.
Guests must not feed wildlife, leave vehicles in restricted zones or cross safety barriers.
Swimming near waterfalls or fast-moving rivers is not recommended.
Attraction fees and operating hours are controlled by third parties and may change without notice.
