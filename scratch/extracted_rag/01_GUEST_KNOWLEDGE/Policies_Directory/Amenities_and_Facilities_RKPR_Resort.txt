1. Overview
RKPR Resort provides recreational, wellness, family, business and accessibility facilities across the property. Facility timings may change
because of maintenance, weather, private events, safety requirements or seasonal operating conditions.
Field
Details
Main facility zones
Lobby and Central Wing, Pool and Fitness Zone, Family Courtyard, Garden and Recreation Zone, Business Wing
and Private Villa Zone
Booking principle
Facilities marked 'booking required' must be reserved before use. Complimentary facilities may still require a time
slot during high occupancy
Guest conduct
Guests must follow staff instructions, posted safety signs and capacity limits
Children
Children must be supervised wherever a facility specifies adult supervision
Maintenance closure
A temporarily closed facility cannot be guaranteed as part of a stay; an alternative may be offered where practical

3. Accessibility Summary
Field
Details
Step-free public areas
Main lobby, Azure Terrace, Kids' Club, Business Centre, meeting room, prayer room and principal guest paths
Accessible rooms
Selected Garden Deluxe Rooms include roll-in showers, wider doors, grab bars and lowered controls
Accessible parking
Marked bays are located near the main lobby
Wheelchairs
Manual wheelchairs are available subject to availability
Lifts
Main guest wings and business/fitness levels are served by passenger lifts
Pool accessibility
The main pool has a step-free deck and transfer steps but no mechanical pool lift
Assistance request
Guests should share mobility requirements before arrival so suitable rooms, routes and transport can be arranged

5. Frequently Asked Questions
Question
Answer
Do you have a swimming pool?
Yes. RKPR Resort has an infinity pool open from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM and a children's pool open
until 7:00 PM.
Is the gym complimentary?
Yes. The fitness centre is complimentary for resident guests aged 16 and above.
Is Wi-Fi available throughout the resort?
Complimentary Wi-Fi is available in rooms, villas and most public areas.
Do you provide EV charging?
Yes. Limited EV charging points are available by advance reservation and are charged per kWh.
Is the resort wheelchair accessible?
Core public areas are step-free, selected rooms are adapted, lifts serve the main wings and
wheelchairs are available subject to availability.
Do you have a private beach?
No. RKPR Resort is an inland mountain property and does not have a private beach.
Are pets allowed?
No. Pets are not permitted. Certified service animals may be accepted with advance documentation.
Is there a business centre?
Yes. The Business Centre is open from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM and includes workstations and printing
services.
