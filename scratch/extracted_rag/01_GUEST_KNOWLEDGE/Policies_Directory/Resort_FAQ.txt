RKPR Resort FAQ

Q: What time is check-in? A: Check-in begins at 2:00 PM.

Q: What time is check-out? A: Check-out is by 11:00 AM.

Q: Is early check-in available? A: Yes. Early check-in is subject to
room availability. Charges may apply for arrivals before 10:00 AM.

Q: Is late check-out available? A: Yes. Late check-out is subject to
availability and may incur additional charges.

Q: Is Wi-Fi complimentary? A: Yes. Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is
available in all rooms, villas and public areas.

Q: Do you have a swimming pool? A: Yes. The infinity pool is open daily
from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Q: Do you have a spa? A: Yes. Aranya Wellness Spa operates daily from
9:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekdays, and 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekends and
public holidays. ("Cedar Leaf Spa" was the resort's pre-2026 spa name
and is no longer in use; guests should be told the current name is
Aranya Wellness Spa.)

Q: Do I need to book spa treatments in advance? A: Yes. Advance booking
is recommended. Popular treatments should be reserved at least 24 hours
in advance.

Q: How much does a couple massage cost? A: Couple spa rituals start from
INR 11,800 for two guests (the Mountain Serenity for Two), and range up
to INR 18,500 for two depending on the selected treatment.

Q: Are there activities for children? A: Yes. The Kids' Club offers arts
& crafts, treasure hunts, storytelling, movie nights and supervised
indoor games. It is open daily from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM (last entry
6:30 PM) for children aged 4-12. On declared long weekends and festival
periods it may extend to 8:00 PM, subject to staffing confirmation from
the Recreation Manager on the day - this is not guaranteed by default.

Q: Is the nature walk free? A: Yes. The guided morning nature walk is
complimentary for resident guests.

Q: Do I need to book yoga in advance? A: Yes. Yoga sessions should be
reserved before 8:00 PM on the previous day.

Q: Will the bonfire happen if it rains? A: Outdoor bonfires may be
cancelled or moved indoors due to weather.

Q: Which airport is closest? A: Kempegowda International Airport
(Bengaluru) is approximately 190 km away.

Q: Can the resort arrange airport pickup? A: Yes. Airport transfers are
available 24 hours with advance booking.

Q: Can six people travel in one vehicle? A: Yes. A Luxury MUV can
accommodate up to six guests, subject to luggage limits.

Q: Is room service available at midnight? A: Yes. In-room dining
operates 24 hours with a midnight menu from 11:00 PM to 6:30 AM.

Q: Do you serve Jain food? A: Yes. Jain dishes are available across all
dining venues with advance notice for certain preparations.

Q: Do you have vegan and gluten-free options? A: Yes. Vegan and
gluten-free meals are available. Please inform the restaurant about
dietary requirements.

Q: Are pets allowed? A: No. Pets are not permitted, except certified
service animals.

Q: Is smoking allowed? A: Smoking is prohibited inside all rooms and
villas. Designated outdoor smoking areas are available.

Q: Are visitors allowed? A: Yes. Visitors must register with valid ID
and follow resort visitor policies.

Q: Is parking available? A: Yes. Complimentary parking is available for
registered resident guests.

Q: Do you have wheelchair-accessible rooms? A: Yes. Selected rooms are
designed with accessibility features.

Q: Can I celebrate birthdays or anniversaries? A: Yes. Decoration
packages and celebration arrangements are available on request.

Q: Do you provide honeymoon decorations? A: Yes. Romantic room
decoration packages can be arranged with advance booking.

Q: What payment methods are accepted? A: Visa, Mastercard, RuPay,
American Express, UPI, bank transfer and cash within legal limits.

Q: What should I do if my flight is delayed? A: Inform the Transport
Desk. If your flight number was provided, the driver will monitor the
revised arrival time.

Q: Is there a security deposit? A: Yes. A refundable security deposit
may be collected during check-in depending on the accommodation
category.

Q: Can I extend my stay? A: Yes. Extensions are subject to room
availability and prevailing rates.

Q: Where can I see full restaurant menus and prices? A: See
RKPR_Resort_Restaurant_Menu_Full_2026.pdf for the complete, itemised
menu with prices and dietary tags for Azure Terrace, Ember & Spice,
The Cedar Lounge and Summit Bar.

Q: What are the current room rates? A: See
RKPR_Resort_Official_Rate_Card_2026.pdf, the authoritative 2026 rate
source. The older scanned rate cards (Rate cards.png, Scanned
Cards.png) are archived for reference only and should not be quoted
to guests, since they cover the expired 2024-2025 period.

Q: What is the infinity pool's exact description? A: It is an outdoor,
temperature-moderated infinity pool with a valley outlook. It is not
described as "heated."
