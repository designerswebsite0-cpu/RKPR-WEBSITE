6. Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Is early check-in available?
A: Yes, subject to room availability. Charges may apply for arrivals before 10:00 AM.
Q: Is late check-out available?
A: Yes, subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
Q: Are pets allowed?
A: No, except certified service animals.
Q: Is smoking allowed?
A: Smoking is prohibited inside all rooms and villas. Designated outdoor smoking areas are available.
Q: Do you have wheelchair-accessible rooms?
A: Yes - four designated Garden Deluxe Rooms, request in advance.
Q: Is there a security deposit?
A: Yes, a refundable deposit may be collected at check-in depending on accommodation category.
